Barbell Deadlift

4 × 4-5 reps
Rest: 3 min
Muscles🟢 Posterior Chain (glutes, hamstrings, lats, erectors)  ·  🔵 Quads  ·  🔵 Traps

Bar over mid-foot, grip just outside legs. Hinge to bar, big breath into belly, lats engaged ("protect your armpits"). Drive floor away, then thrust hips through at the top. Deadlift is the single best mass builder — it loads the entire posterior chain simultaneously. 4×4-5 is heavy strength work.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

Leg Extension

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 60 sec
Muscles🟢 Rectus Femoris (quad peak isolation)

Full extension at top, 1-sec squeeze, slow 3-sec negative. Leg extension is the only exercise that isolates the rectus femoris (the central quad muscle that creates the visible peak). After the heavy deadlift, legs get knee-dominant isolation work that squats cannot provide. Go light enough to feel the quad, not the hip flexors.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Terminal Knee Extension (TKE)Band around knee, straighten leg. Same stimulus. 3×15-20.
Wall SitHold for 45-60 sec. 3 rounds.
Step-Up (slow lowering)Control the descent to maximize quad work. 3×12-15 each leg.

Seated Dumbbell OHP

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 2 min
Muscles🟢 Anterior + Medial Delt  ·  🔵 Tricep  ·  🔵 Upper Trap

Seated removes leg drive — pure shoulder work. Start at ear height, press to full lockout overhead. Wednesday OHP vs Monday cable lateral: OHP builds delt mass and strength, laterals build width. Both are needed for complete shoulder development. Don't arch your back — stay tall.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

Machine Shoulder PressGood for removing stabilizer fatigue. Same sets/reps.
Pike Push-UpHips high, lower head toward floor. 3×10-15.

Seated Cable Row

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 2 min
Muscles🟢 Lower Lats  ·  🟢 Rhomboids  ·  🔵 Bicep  ·  🔵 Rear Delt

Sit tall, handle pulled to lower chest with elbows close to ribs. Hold the contraction 1 second — squeeze the shoulder blades together. Seated cable row targets lower lat insertion and rhomboids distinctly from Monday's wide pulldown (upper lat width) and Tuesday's barbell row (mid thickness). Three different row angles = complete back.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

DB Chest-Supported RowIncline bench, DBs hanging. Same sets/reps.
Resistance Band RowSeated on floor, band around feet. 3×15-20.

Preacher Curl

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Bicep Short Head (width)  ·  🔵 Brachialis

Upper arm braced flat on the pad — eliminates all cheat. Lower to near-full extension (don't hyperextend). The preacher position flexes the shoulder, shorting the long head and maximally targeting the short head — which adds bicep width. Monday hits long head peak, Tuesday hits brachialis thickness, Wednesday hits short head width. Complete bicep development.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
DB Preacher CurlOne arm at a time on a preacher bench or incline bench. Same sets/reps.
Scott Curl (EZ-bar)Same as preacher with EZ-bar. Same sets/reps.
Concentration CurlElbow braced on inner thigh. 3×12-15 each arm.

V-Bar Pushdown

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Tricep Medial Head  ·  🔵 Tricep Lateral Head

V-bar attachment, elbows pinned. The angled grip shifts slightly more load to the medial head vs the rope pushdown (Monday's lateral head) and overhead extension (Tuesday's long head). Three pushdown variations across the week targets all three tricep heads. Keep elbows still — move at the elbow joint only.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

Dumbbell Overhead ExtensionOne arm at a time. 3×12-15 each.
Bench DipFeet elevated for more load. 3×12-15.

Plank + Dead Bug

3 rounds
Rest: 60 sec
Muscles🟢 Transverse Abdominis  ·  🟢 Rectus Abdominis  ·  🔵 Hip Flexors

Plank: forearms down, body rigid, hold 30-40 seconds. Immediately move to Dead Bug: on back, arm and opposite leg extend slowly while lower back stays flat on floor. 8 reps each side. Core stability improves squat and deadlift performance. Use the weight input below to log your plank hold time in seconds.
