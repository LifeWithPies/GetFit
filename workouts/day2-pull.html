Romanian Deadlift

4 × 8-10 reps
Rest: 2.5 min
Muscles🟢 Hamstrings (hip-dominant)  ·  🔵 Glutes  ·  🔵 Spinal Erectors

Push hips back as bar slides down your thighs. Stop when you feel a deep hamstring stretch (around shin height). Knees stay slightly bent throughout. Drive hips forward to stand. RDL hits hamstrings in a hip-dominant pattern — different fibre stimulus than the knee-dominant squat on Monday.

Incline Dumbbell Press

4 × 8-10 reps
Rest: 2 min
Muscles🟢 Upper Chest (clavicular head)  ·  🔵 Anterior Delt

Set bench to 30-45°. Lower DBs wide — feel deep upper chest stretch at bottom. Press to full extension. The incline shifts load to the clavicular pec head — the upper chest that creates the shelf look from collarbone down. Tuesday = incline, Monday = flat: complete chest coverage across the week.

Barbell Bent-Over Row

3 × 8-10 reps
Rest: 2 min
Muscles🟢 Mid-Back Thickness (lats, rhomboids)  ·  🔵 Bicep  ·  🔵 Rear Delt

Hinge to ~45°, overhand grip shoulder-width. Pull bar to lower chest — elbows track close to ribs. Squeeze shoulder blades together for 1 sec at top. Horizontal pulling targets mid-back thickness distinctly from Monday's vertical pulldown. Heavy rows build the dense, thick back look.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

Face Pull

3 × 15-20 reps
Rest: 60 sec
Muscles🟢 Posterior Delt  ·  🟢 External Rotators (rotator cuff)

Cable at face height, rope attachment. Pull toward your forehead with elbows HIGH and flaring outward. Counterbalances all pressing volume accumulated across the week. Keep it light — form over load. Skipping this consistently leads to shoulder impingement over time.

Hammer Curl

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Brachialis (under-bicep thickness)  ·  🟢 Brachioradialis  ·  🔵 Bicep

Neutral grip, thumbs up throughout — don't rotate the wrist. The brachialis sits beneath the bicep; when developed it pushes the bicep up and makes the arm look thicker from the side. Monday hits long head (incline curl); Tuesday hits brachialis (hammer). Together they build complete arm width and peak.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

EZ-Bar Curl (close grip)Neutral-ish angle. Same sets/reps.

Overhead EZ-Bar Extension

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Tricep Long Head (mass)  ·  🔵 Tricep Medial Head

Seated or standing. Elbows pointed up, lower bar behind head slowly. The long head crosses the shoulder joint — the overhead position provides maximum long-head stretch, producing more hypertrophy here than pushdowns. Long head is the largest tricep portion. Control the negative, don't let elbows flare.

Equipment not available? See alternatives

Close-Grip Bench PressHands shoulder-width on bar. 3×10-12.

Seated Calf Raise

3 × 15-20 reps
Rest: 60 sec
Muscles🟢 Soleus (deep calf — lower leg thickness)

Soleus is maximally active when the knee is bent — the gastrocnemius goes slack. Full ROM: heel as low as possible, pause, rise fully. Alternating standing calf (gastrocnemius, Mon/Wed/Fri) with seated (soleus, Tue/Thu) ensures complete calf development across the week.
