Back Squat

4 × 5 reps
Rest: 3 min
Muscles🟢 Quads (full sweep)  ·  🔵 Glutes  ·  🔵 Hamstrings

Bar on mid-traps, feet shoulder-width. Big breath into belly, brace core, sit back and down to parallel. Drive through the full foot — don't let knees cave. The squat builds overall quad mass uniformly. 4×5 is strength work — go heavy and track progressive overload each week.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Leg Press (hip-width)Full ROM, control the descent. 4×8-10.
Goblet SquatDumbbell at chest, great for depth. 4×10-12.
Smith Machine SquatWhen barbell racks are taken. Same sets/reps.

Barbell Bench Press

4 × 6-8 reps
Rest: 2.5 min
Muscles🟢 Mid Chest (sternal head)  ·  🔵 Anterior Delt  ·  🔵 Tricep

Retract and depress shoulder blades before every rep. Lower bar to mid-chest in 3 seconds — this eccentric loading drives hypertrophy. Drive explosively up. Flat bench maximally loads the sternal pec head. Monday is flat bench day — track weight, aim to add 5 lb when you hit 8 reps all 4 sets.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Dumbbell Bench PressGreater ROM at bottom. Use ~60% of bar weight. Same sets/reps.
Machine Chest PressReduce load ~15%. Same sets/reps.
Weighted Push-UpPlate on upper back. 4×12-15 slow reps.

Wide-Grip Lat Pulldown

3 × 8-10 reps
Rest: 2 min
Muscles🟢 Upper Lats (V-taper width)  ·  🔵 Bicep  ·  🔵 Rear Delt

Grip 1.5× shoulder-width. Lean back 10-15°, pull bar to upper chest by driving elbows down and back. Wide grip maximizes upper lat recruitment — this builds the V-taper width. Control the full return to arm extension for complete lat stretch.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Wide-Grip Pull-UpBest substitute. Add weight belt if needed. Same sets/reps.
Assisted Pull-Up MachineBuild toward bodyweight pull-ups. Same sets/reps.
Single-Arm Cable PulldownGreater stretch at top. 3×10-12 each arm.

Cable Lateral Raise

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Medial Delt (shoulder width)  ·  🔵 Anterior Delt

Cable provides tension from 0° — dumbbells give almost none at the start. Lead with elbow, not wrist. Raise to shoulder height. Slow 3-second negative. Do both arms cross-body for maximum stretch. Wide capped delts are built here.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Dumbbell Lateral RaiseSlight forward lean, lead with elbow. Same sets/reps.
Machine Lateral RaiseSeated, controlled. Same sets/reps.
Band Lateral RaiseStep on band. 3×20.

Incline Dumbbell Curl

3 × 10-12 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Bicep Long Head (peak height)  ·  🔵 Brachialis

Set bench to 60°. Arms hang fully behind torso — this pre-stretches the long head beyond what standing curls achieve. Curl slowly, squeeze hard at top. Long head creates bicep peak. Don't swing; if you need momentum the weight is too heavy.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Standing DB CurlFull supination at top. Same sets/reps.
Low-Cable CurlConstant tension. 3×12-15.
Drag CurlBar drags up torso — excellent long-head tension. Same sets/reps.

Rope Tricep Pushdown

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 90 sec
Muscles🟢 Tricep Lateral Head (horseshoe shape)  ·  🔵 Tricep Medial Head

Pin elbows to ribs. At full extension flare rope ends outward for peak lateral-head contraction. Return in 3 seconds for full stretch. Triceps = 2/3 of upper arm mass. The lateral head gives the horseshoe shape visible from the front.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
V-Bar PushdownMore medial head emphasis. Same sets/reps.
Tricep KickbackHinge forward, extend arm behind hip. 3×12-15 each.
Diamond Push-UpHands together under chest. 3×max reps.

Standing Calf Raise

3 × 12-15 reps
Rest: 60 sec
Muscles🟢 Gastrocnemius (upper calf mass)  ·  🔵 Soleus

Full range only — heel as low as possible, 2-sec pause at the stretch, rise to peak contraction. Gastrocnemius is maximally active with the knee straight. No bouncing — calves respond to time under tension, not speed.

Equipment not available? See alternatives
Leg Press Calf RaiseFeet low on platform. Same sets/reps.
Single-Leg BW Calf RaiseOn a step, add dumbbell for load. 3×20 each leg.
Smith Machine Calf RaiseBar on traps. Same sets/reps.
